Tools for generating and managing dynamic missions and campaigns.

---

### [DCS Retribution](https://github.com/dcs-retribution/dcs-retribution)
**Release:** [v1.4.1](https://github.com/dcs-retribution/dcs-retribution/releases/tag/v1.4.1)
Active community fork of DCS Liberation. Turn-based dynamic campaign engine — manages a persistent strategic layer with logistics, reinforcements, and air superiority over time. More actively developed than the original Liberation.

---

### [DCS Liberation](https://github.com/dcs-liberation/dcs_liberation)
**Release:** [14.1.0](https://github.com/dcs-liberation/dcs_liberation/releases/tag/14.1.0)
Original turn-based single-player/co-op dynamic campaign. Generates complete DCS missions from a persistent combat environment. Retribution is the preferred fork for new installs.

---

### [Briefing Room](https://github.com/DCS-BR-Tools/briefing-room-for-dcs)
**Release:** [release-2026.02.17-20260217-135029-22100948806-1](https://github.com/DCS-BR-Tools/briefing-room-for-dcs/releases/tag/release-2026.02.17-20260217-135029-22100948806-1)
Advanced quick mission generator. Creates complete scenarios across a wide range of mission types and theaters. Substantially more capable than ED's built-in fast mission generator.

---

### [DCT](https://github.com/jtoppins/dct)
**Release:** [v0.11.2](https://github.com/jtoppins/dct/releases/tag/v0.11.2)
Dynamic Campaign Tools. Lua-based framework for persistent server-side campaigns. Mission designers define templates and tasks; DCT manages state between server restarts.

- **Docs:** https://jtoppins.github.io/dct/

---

### [DSMC](https://github.com/Chromium18/DSMC1)
**Release:** [v1.4.4.3394](https://github.com/Chromium18/DSMC1/releases/tag/v1.4.4.3394)
Dynamic Sequential Mission Campaign. Saves the current mission state (unit alive/dead status, positions, warehouses) and generates a new `.miz` file for continuation. Compatible with MOOSE, MIST, and CTLD.

---

### [DCS Olympus](https://github.com/Pax1601/DCSOlympus)
**Release:** [v2.0.5](https://github.com/Pax1601/DCSOlympus/releases/tag/v2.0.5)
Real-time RTS-style web map for DCS servers. Game masters spawn, move, task, and remove units live during a mission — similar to Arma's Zeus. Integrates with DCSServerBot.
