# Aircraft Mods

Free community aircraft mods. No FC3 required unless noted.

---

### [Community A-4E-C Skyhawk](https://github.com/Community-A-4E/community-a4e-c)
**Release:** [v2.3](https://github.com/Community-A-4E/community-a4e-c/releases/tag/v2.3)
Full-fidelity A-4E with clickable cockpit, air-to-ground radar, carrier operations, and EFM. The gold standard of DCS community mods. Open source.

---

### [Super Hercules — Anubis Expanded](https://github.com/SkzKatsushiro/DCS-Hercules-Anubis-Mod-Expanded)
**Release:** [check pending](https://github.com/SkzKatsushiro/DCS-Hercules-Anubis-Mod-Expanded/releases/latest)
C-130J with clickable cockpit, ramp panel, custom flight model, HUD, and 4 manned crew positions. One of the most capable utility aircraft mods.
